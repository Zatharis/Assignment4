Infected Mushroom

About the band

Infected mushrooms is a duo from Israeli, they make psytrance music. their duo formed in 1996 and has made many albums since.

A brief history

Infected Mushroom was formed in 1996 by Erez Eisen and Amit Duvdevani. They gained popularity in the late 1990s and early 2000s with their unique blend of psytrance and other genres.
